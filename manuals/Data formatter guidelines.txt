WyntrAI TRAINING DATA FORMATTER GUIDELINES

CONTROLS:
    1. SELECT AND MERGE
        1. --shift + [[num]-user_[name_x]] + [[num]-user_[name_x]] + [...]
        2. --enter
        merges them. (VISUAL_2)
    2. SELECT AND REMOVE
        1. --shift + [[num]-user_[name_x/y]] + [...]
        2. --backspace
        removes them. (VISUAL_3)
    3. SELECT AND PAIR
        1. --shift + [[num]-user_[name_x]] + [[num]-user_[name_y]]
        2. --enter
        pairs them. (VISUAL_4)

    4. --ctrl + --z to undo
    5. --ctrl + --y to redo
    6. --ctrl + click a message to edit
    7. --ctrl + click a pair to mark export root

    8. "import JSON" to merge formatted dataset with yours
    9. "Export JSON" to download formatted datasets
    10. "auto pair" to automatically pair
